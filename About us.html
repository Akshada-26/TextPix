About Us

"See, Hear, Understand!"

"TextPix" is an innovative app uniting image recognition and text-to-speech technology. Our mission is universal accessibility for visual content. Snap a photo or select an image, and TextPix swiftly identifies objects, scenes, and text within it. It generates descriptive text, benefiting individuals with visual impairments and providing valuable context for all users.

But TextPix goes beyond text; it offers a user-friendly text-to-speech feature, allowing you to listen to descriptions in a clear, natural voice. Our commitment to innovation and accessibility drives us to continually enhance TextPix. Join us in our mission to foster inclusivity and create a more accessible digital world with TextPix.
